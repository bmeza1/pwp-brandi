Content Strategy

  • Welcome: This section will include a brief introduction about what I do and how I can help my ideal client. To the right of the section, I will include links to my social media profiles: GitHub, LinkedIn, Instagram. The links will be represented by icons. The welcome message and social media links will be overlaid on a professional background image of me.
  • About Me: This section will highlight my related professional experiences, core skills set and a few hobbies of mine to showcase my personality. To the right of the paragraphs, I will include a feature similar to an accordion that will highlight the programming languages and frameworks I learned in the bootcamp. The tone of the about me section will be personable and casual.
  • Portfolio: This section will include 4-6 examples of my best work. The gallery will contain animated, responsive images that give a sneak-peak into the project. For each project I will include my role and contributions.
  • Contact: The contact section will contain a contact form and my professional email address.
