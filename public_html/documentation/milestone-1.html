PWP Milestone One

Purpose, Audience, & Goal

  • Purpose: This project will be a professional portfolio website with a focus on social media marketing and website design for businesses in the health and beauty industries.
  • Audience: This site is designed with a professional audience in mind and is targeted to businesses owners and managers within the health and beauty industries nationwide. Target audience to include: owners, hiring managers, peers, and other marketing agencies.
  • Goal: The primary goal of this site is for prospective clients and peers to initiate contact with me in regard to employment and collaboration opportunities. Users of the site can contact me through the contact form and/or the connected social media profiles. The secondary goal is to showcase and promote my work.

Persona

  • Name: Lydia Rodriguez
  • Age: 35
  • Profession: Owner and manager of Bliss Beauty, a new spa in Albuquerque, NM. Lydia is looking to hire a freelance social media marketing manager to design and execute social media campaigns, track KPI's and manage all social media platforms. Lydia also needs a website for her new business. The spa specializes in massage, facials, and body contouring.
  • Technology: Mac casual user. Uses a 2020 Macbook Air laptop, an iPhone 11 and a Chrome browser.
  • Attitudes and Behaviors: Lydia is a busy business owner. In addition to managing a staff of massage therapists and estheticians, she also sees clients daily. Lydia spends her short 30-minute lunch break reviewing potential candidates for the open position. She doesn't love social media but understands that it is essential for attracting clients and builidng relationships.
  • Frustrations and Needs: Lydia needs to hire someone with at least 3 years of experience and prefers a candidate with a bachelor's degree. She is looking for someone reliable, dedicated to their work, and someone who with a good personality who will match the cultural fit at Bliss Beauty. Lydia has received a ton of resumes, but still feels she hasn't found the perfect candidate. Bliss Beauty is still a new business but Lydia is willing to pay a competitive rate. She must hire within 2 weeks.
  • Goals: Lydia is concerned with finding the right website designer and social media marketing manager for her business. She is short on time and needs to decide within 5 minutes of opening a website if this person is worthy of an interview.
  • Use Story: As a user, I need to determine whether this person is the right candidate to create my website and manage my businesses social media profiles.
