PWP Milestone 1

Purpose, Audience, & Goal

  • Purpose: This project will be a professional portfolio website with a focus on social media marketing and website design for businesses in the health and beauty industries.
  • Audience: This site is designed with a professional audience in mind and is targeted to businesses owners and managers within the health and beauty industries nationwide. Target audience to include: owners, hiring managers, peers, and other marketing agencies.
  • Goal: The primary goal of this site is for prospective clients and peers to initiate contact with me in regard to employment and collaboration opportunities. The secondary goal is to showcase and promote my work.
